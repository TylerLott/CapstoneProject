About the Team

We are a group of undergraduate seniors in the Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Department at Utah State Universit. Our interests range from aerodynamics and controls to machine learning. The project we were tasked with was to build a leveling system for legacy tripod systems to speed up the deployment time of the systems. We were extremely challeged by the project and were able to learn a significant amount.

We would like to give L3Harris a huge THANK YOU for giving us this oportunity.
